### 1. The Warm Intro Protocol‚Ñ¢ ‚≠ê PRIORITY
**Video Length:** 8-10 min

**The 4-Step Process:**

### 1. The Me-in-We Extractor‚Ñ¢ ‚≠ê PRIORITY

### 2. The Impact Bullet Builder‚Ñ¢ ‚≠ê PRIORITY
**Video Length:** 8-10 min
**The Formula:** [Task] + [Metric] + [How] + [Why] = IMPACT
**Components:**
- Task: What you accomplished
- Metric: Quantifiable proof
- How: Your specific approach
- Why: Business impact
**Complete Example:**
- Before: "Maintained spreadsheets for test results."
- After: "Solely managed data tracking for 3 major programs across a 10-person team, handling 100+ daily tests. Developed custom Python automation touching the Azure DevOps API, cutting maintenance time by 85%..."

### 3. The Boring-to-Standout Bullet Upgrade‚Ñ¢

### 4. The Universal Translator + ATS Pass‚Ñ¢

### 7. The Domain Translator‚Ñ¢
