The Resource Audit
30-Minute Activation Protocol
Spend 30 minutes brainstorming every portal, office, club, or connection you've heard of but ignored.
Track in a simple spreadsheet:
  • Resource: Career center, alumni portal, ASME chapter, Professor Smith
  • Contact: Name/email of who to reach
  • Ask Made: What you requested (intro, advice, resume review)
  • Outcome: Response, referral, interview, etc.
