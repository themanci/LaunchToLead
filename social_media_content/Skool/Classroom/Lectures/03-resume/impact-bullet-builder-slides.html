Key Principles
1
Quantify whenever possible
Use %, $, time saved, people impacted, scale metrics

What changed because of your work? Not just what you did.
3
Use strong action verbs
Designed, Implemented, Reduced, Increased, Led, Optimized
4
Add technical specificity
Tools, languages, frameworks, methodologies you used
