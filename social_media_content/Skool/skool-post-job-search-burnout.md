## Why This Happens

It's not your fault. It's one of four things:

1. **Spray-and-Pray:** You're applying to 100 jobs with 1-2% response rates. Brutal effort-to-outcome ratio.

2. **No Visibility:** Without tracking where you applied and what stage you're in, anxiety loops forever. You're always wondering "did they respond?"

3. **Obsessive Checking:** Refreshing email 20x per day feels like work. It's not. It's feeding anxiety.

4. **Solo Grinding:** Without accountability, every rejection feels personal. The grind feels isolating.

---
