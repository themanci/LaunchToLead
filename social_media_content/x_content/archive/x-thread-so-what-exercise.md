## Tweet 4 (The Exercise)
The "So What" Exercise:

1. Write down every "what if" haunting you
2. For each one, ask: "So what? Then what happens?"
3. Keep going: "And then what?"
4. Follow the chain to the very end

Don't stop until you reach the final conclusion.
