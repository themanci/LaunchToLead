Interview Strategy

Same Intent. Softer Delivery.

❌ Don't Ask This

"What's the biggest mistake you've made in your career?"

Puts the interviewer on the hot seat. Feels like a challenge, not a conversation.

✅ Ask This Instead

"You've been here 15 years — what's one thing you wish someone told you when you first started?"

Same insight. Same curiosity. But it feels like genuine interest, not an interrogation.

You want them to leave thinking "I liked that person" — not "That was uncomfortable."
