Interview Prep Tip

"What would you do differently if you could do it again?"

Don't wait for them to ask.
Tell them first.

✓ Shows you're reflective, not just doing
✓ Shows you will improve over time
✓ Shows humility—you know you're not perfect

For every example you prepare, know: What would Version 2.0 look like?
