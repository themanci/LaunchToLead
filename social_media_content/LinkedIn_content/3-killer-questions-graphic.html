Interview Strategy

3 Questions to Ask
at the End of Every Interview

1

Address Concerns

"Are there any gaps in my qualifications that might make this role more challenging for me?"

2

Assess Their Investment in You

"What does your onboarding or training process look like for someone new to the team?"

3

Get Tactical

"What tools, technologies, or systems will I be working with day-to-day?"
