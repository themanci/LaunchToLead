Turn Meeting Hell Into Something Better

Small shifts that made meetings way less painful. Nothing groundbreaking—just things that actually work.

40% of focus is lost to context switching. Better meetings = fewer meetings overall.

4 Things That Help

1
Send a Clear Agenda
Goal, decision, info needed—send 24h ahead.
2
Actually Facilitate
Call on people directly. No silent spectators.
3
Put Away the Laptops
Be fully present or decline the meeting.
4
End with Actions
Who does what by when? Follow up in writing.
Try one of these in your next meeting. LAUNCHTOLEAD.COM
