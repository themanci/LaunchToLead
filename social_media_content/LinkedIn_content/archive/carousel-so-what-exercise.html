Example: Following the chain

FEAR: "What if I get laid off in a year?"
SO WHAT: "I'd have to find another job."
THEN: "Do you have an emergency fund?"
ANSWER: "Yes, 6-9 months of expenses."
THEN: "So your family won't be homeless?"
ANSWER: "No. We'd have runway."
