The Antidote
1
Build a Sustainable Pace
5-10 targeted applications per week, not 50. Quality > volume. You're in this for 68 days, not burning out in week 3.
2
Track Everything
Spreadsheet of every app, stage, next action date. Visibility removes anxiety. You can see progress.
3
Time-Box Your Search
Job searching 9-11 AM and 2-4 PM. Then stop. Don't live on LinkedIn. Don't check email every 15 mins.
4
Lean On Community
Weekly check-in with accountability partner. Share wins (interviews, referrals). Share struggles. Don't do this alone.
