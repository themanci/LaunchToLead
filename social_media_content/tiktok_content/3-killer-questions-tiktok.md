# 3 Killer Questions to Ask - TikTok/Reels/Shorts Script

**Platform:** TikTok, Instagram Reels, Facebook Reels, YouTube Shorts
**Style:** Alex Hormozi-inspired ‚Äî punchy, direct, value-dense, retention hooks

## SCRIPT

**[HOOK - First 1-2 seconds]**
"Stop asking 'What's the company culture like?'"

---

**[PATTERN INTERRUPT - Seconds 2-5]**
"That question tells them nothing about you ‚Äî and tells you nothing useful about them."

"Here are the 3 questions I tell every engineer to ask instead."

---

**[QUESTION 1 - Seconds 5-15]**
"Number one: 'Are there any gaps in my qualifications that might make this role more challenging for me?'"

"Yes, it's uncomfortable. That's why it works."

"You're giving them permission to voice concerns ‚Äî and giving yourself a chance to handle them before you leave the room."

---

**[QUESTION 2 - Seconds 15-25]**
"Number two: 'What does your onboarding or training process look like?'"

"This tells you if they actually invest in new hires ‚Äî or if they're gonna throw you in the deep end with no support."

"If they stumble on this one? Red flag."

---

**[QUESTION 3 - Seconds 25-35]**
"Number three: 'What tools or technologies will I be working with day-to-day?'"

"Simple. Practical. Shows you're already thinking about the work."

---

**[THE REFRAME - Seconds 35-45]**
"Why only three?"

"For a phone screen, three is enough. More than that and you seem difficult. Less than that and you seem unprepared."

"Your goal right now isn't to interrogate them. It's to get an offer."

"Once you HAVE the offer ‚Äî that's when you ask everything. Leave no stone unturned."

---

**[CLOSE - Seconds 45-50]**
"But you can't negotiate from a position of strength until you have an offer in hand."

"Get the offer first."

## PRODUCTION NOTES

- **Pacing:** Fast but clear. Pause briefly after each question for emphasis.
- **Energy:** Confident, direct. You're giving them the cheat code.
- **Eye contact:** Direct to camera the whole time.
- **Gestures:** Count on fingers for the 3 questions. Point at camera on "Get the offer first."
- **Edit style:** Jump cuts on filler words. Keep it tight. No dead air.
