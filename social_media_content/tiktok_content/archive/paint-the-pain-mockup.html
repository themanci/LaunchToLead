Suggested Script

Hook (0:00-0:05)

"Stop glossing over the 'Situation' in your interviews. You are killing your own story before it even starts."

The Mistake (0:05-0:15)

"Most engineers do this: 'Situation: We had a bug. Task: I had to fix it.'
(Shake head) Boring. Low stakes. I'm already checking my email."

The Fix (0:15-0:30)

"You need to Paint the Pain. Don't lie, but emphasize the stakes.
Try this instead: 'Situation: A critical latency bug was threatening a $50k contract penalty just 48 hours before launch.'"

The Psychology (0:30-0:45)

"See the difference? Risk. Money. Urgency. Now I'm listening.
Remember: The bigger the monster, the bigger the hero. If the problem sounds small, your solution feels cheap."

CTA (0:45-0:60)
