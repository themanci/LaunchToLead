Verbatim Script - Say This Exactly

SCENE 1: THE ASK (0-5s)

Interviewer asks you a behavioral question. You say: "I want to make sure I give you a really relevant example. Could you clarify what you mean by leadership in this context?"

SCENE 2: THE FEAR (5-10s)

[Act this out dramatically] What you think will happen: "HOW DARE YOU ask for clarification! You FAIL this interview AUTOMATICALLY! Security!" [Pause] Yeah... that's not real.

SCENE 3: REALITY (10-15s)

What actually happens? The interviewer says "Oh sure, I mean any time you stepped up to guide a team through something difficult." And now you can give them exactly what they're looking for.

SCENE 4: YOUR TOOLKIT (15-25s)

Here's what you can say in any interview: "Could you repeat that?" "Could you clarify what you mean by...?" "Could I have a different question in this same area?" "Can we come back to this one at the end?" Or just: "Give me a moment. I want to think of a really strong example." One to two minutes of silence? Totally fine.

SCENE 5: THE PUNCHLINE (25-35s)

Nervous candidates blurt out any answer and hope it's good enough. Confident candidates take the time to understand the question and deliver a real example. You're not desperate to pass. You're proving you actually have the competency. That's maturity. That's confidence. That's what gets the offer.
