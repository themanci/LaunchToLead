### THE PROBLEM (0:15-0:35)

- But they can't articulate *what's* wrong

### WHY THIS MATTERS (0:35-0:55)

### STEP 1: MINE YOUR FEEDBACK (0:55-1:25)
- You already have dataâ€”use it
- Look at performance reviews for recurring themes
- What do managers consistently say?
- What patterns follow you across jobs?
- If 3 managers said the same thing... it's probably you

### STEP 2: PUSH FOR FEEDBACK (1:25-1:55)
- "My manager never gives me feedback" â†’ that's a problem too
- Ask directly: "What's ONE thing I should work on for the next level?"
- Make it impossible to dodge
- If they can't answer â†’ they're not paying attention or not doing their job

### STEP 3: 360 FEEDBACK (1:55-2:25)
- Get perspective from all directions:
  - Seniors: "What would you improve if you were me?"
  - Peers: "What patterns do you notice?"
  - Juniors: "How could I support you better?"
- Different levels see different blind spots

### WHY PEOPLE SKIP THIS (2:25-2:45)
- Never ask for feedback
- Dismiss it as biased/unfair
- Hear it but don't internalize
- Scared of what they'll learn
- Discomfort now < years of stagnation

### CTA (2:45-3:00)
- Know your gaps before you get a coach
- Know where you want to grow
- Know what's holding you back
- Then make coaching count
