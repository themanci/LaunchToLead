Common Questions

What if I don't have much experience?

Not a problem. Most engineers that I've worked with undervalue the experience they have, they just need someone to help them see it. Also, there are all kinds of independent projects you can work on to make your own experience if necessary. We can brainstorm together.

What engineering majors do you work with?

All of them. The frameworks I will teach you are not specific to one discipline or domain of engineering. They can be applied to any of them equally effectively.

Why 8 weeks?

Because anything worth doing is worth doing right. When planning to set you up for career success, it will need a little more than just a band aid. 8 weeks gives us the proper time to work through all the knowledge and application of that knowledge to make you a dangerous engineering candidate!

What's the guarantee?

If at any point you're not happy with the content or the experience, I'll buy the program back from you at full price — no questions asked. And if any mission is still unclear after 8 weeks, I'll work with you 1:1 for free until it's clear and complete.

Who is this for?

Engineers who have graduated and are struggling to land that first great engineering role — whether you're not getting callbacks, getting interviews but not offers, or you just want to do this right from the start. If you've put in real effort and it's still not producing results, this program was built for you.
