Common Questions

What if I don't have much experience?

Not a problem. Most engineers that I've worked with undervalue the experience they have, they just need someone to help them see it. Also, there are all kinds of independent projects you can work on to make your own experience if necessary. We can brainstorm together.

What engineering majors do you work with?

All of them. The frameworks I will teach you are not specific to one discipline or domain of engineering. They can be applied to any of them equally effectively.

How far in advance should I start?

Ideally you are already practicing for interviews a few weeks before you need to interview, so getting your resume in order, preparing interview examples and starting to work through your strategy would be something ideally you do 3-6 months before a career fair, recruiting event, or before you really start to ratchet up sending out job applications.
