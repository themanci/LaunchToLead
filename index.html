The Obstacle

Invisible Impact

Great work that nobody outside your team knows about doesn't build a promotion case. Your impact needs to reach the people who aren't in the room when the work happens.

The Obstacle

The Milestone

Trusted By Seniors → senior leaders who advocate for you when the promotion decision is being made, not just approve of your work.

Common Questions

What if my manager isn't the problem?

Most of the time it's not one person being unfair — it's that your impact hasn't reached the people who actually decide on promotions. We work on making sure the right people see and remember your work, not just your manager.

What if I'm not sure I even deserve a promotion yet?

That's exactly what we figure out together. We'll look honestly at where your work already operates at the next level and where it still needs to grow, so you have a real, credible case instead of a guess.

Does this work for any engineering discipline?

Yes. The frameworks for visibility, impact, and building senior trust aren't specific to one discipline — they apply whether you're mechanical, software, electrical, or anything in between.

Why 6 months?

Building real visibility and trust with senior leaders takes more than a quick fix. The first 12 weeks build your Promotion-Ready Plan, and the next 12 weeks are about applying it at your real job with support the whole way.

What's the guarantee?

If you complete the first 6 missions and still don't have a clear Promotion-Ready Plan, I'll buy the program back from you at full price — no questions asked.

Who is this for?

Engineers who are getting decent-to-good performance reviews but still aren't getting promoted. If your manager keeps telling you "you're doing great" and it's still not translating into the next title, this program was built for you.
