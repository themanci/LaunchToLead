üìù Teaching Script

üìù Numbered Roadmap Script

"When you start rambling, interviewers lose track. The fix is simple: open your Action section with a number. Say 'I took 4 key actions to address this. First... Second...' This forces them to listen for structure and keeps YOU from getting lost."

üìù Impact Ladder Script

"Most candidates stop at 'I solved the problem.' That's Level 1 ‚Äî the minimum. Level 2 is 'I automated it so it won't happen again.' Level 3 is 'I trained the team and it became the new SOP.' Push every Result up the ladder."

üéØ Live Exercise (5 min each)
