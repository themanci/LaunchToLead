üé§ Teaching Script

The Impact Bullet Builder‚Ñ¢

Minutes 25-45 | Slide 6

[Task] + [Metric] + [How] + [Why] = IMPACT

üé§ Teaching Script

"This is the formula that turns weak bullets into interview-getters. Let's break down each component with YOUR experience. [Pick a bullet] Okay, what was the TASK? Good. Now, what METRIC can we attach to this? How did you do it? And why did it matter to the business?"

Formula Components

  • Task: What you accomplished
  • Metric: Quantifiable proof (%, $, time, scale)
  • How: Your specific approach/tools
  • Why: Business impact/outcome

After: "Solely managed data tracking for 3 major programs across a 10-person team, handling 100+ daily tests. Developed custom Python automation touching the Azure DevOps API, cutting maintenance time by 85% and enabling daily executive visibility into quality metrics."

üîß Common Situations & Responses

"I don't have any metrics."

Use Hidden Metrics Finder. Calculate time savings, estimate scale, focus on scope instead of dollars.

"My experience is all in one industry."

Use Domain Translator. Move up the abstraction ladder. Verbs stay the same, nouns change.

"I only have school projects, no real experience."

School projects ARE experience. Use Me-in-We to claim your role. Use Impact Bullet formula on project outcomes.

"I feel like I'm bragging."

Reframe: You're not bragging, you're accurately representing your contributions. Underselling hurts your chances.
