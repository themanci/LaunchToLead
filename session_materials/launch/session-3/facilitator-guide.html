Immediate Debrief (20 min)

Debrief Flow

  1. 1. Break character: "Okay, let's step out of interview mode. Great job!"
  2. 2. Self-assessment first: "How did that feel? What went well? What was challenging?"
  3. 3. Share positives: Lead with 3-4 specific things they did well
  4. 4. Share improvements: Be specific and actionable, not vague
  5. 5. Identify top 3: Summarize the three highest-impact areas to refine

Facilitator Script

"Alright, let's step out of interview mode. Nice work! Before I share my observations, I want to hear from you‚Äîhow did that feel? What moments felt strong? What caught you off guard?"

[Let client self-assess first]

"Great self-awareness. Here's what I noticed..."

[Share specific positive feedback first, then improvements]

"If I were to summarize the three highest-impact things to focus on before your next interview, they would be: [Top 3 Action Items]"

‚ö†Ô∏è Debrief Best Practices

  • ‚Ä¢ Be specific: "When you answered the teamwork question, you..." not "Your answers were good"
  • ‚Ä¢ Balance positive and constructive: Aim for 60% positive, 40% constructive
  • ‚Ä¢ Make it actionable: "Next time, try..." not just "That didn't work"
  • ‚Ä¢ Don't overwhelm: Focus on top 3 improvements, not everything
  • ‚Ä¢ Build confidence: End on a high note

  • ‚Ä¢ Decouple self-worth from outcomes
