## Methodology Being Tested

This job search is validating the **Launch to Lead** methodology:

1. **Impact Equation** - Every bullet follows: Accomplishment + Evidence/Metric + How + Why
2. **Universal Translator** - Industry jargon converted to universal competencies
3. **Me-in-We Extraction** - Individual contributions isolated from team achievements
4. **Competency Radar** - Job posting analyzed for hidden requirements
5. **Boring to Booming** - Task descriptions transformed to impact statements
