Performance Notes

Hook: Say "How's the job hunt going?" in a mocking relative voice. Make it cringe. Pause after. Let it sit.

Pain Stack: Slow down here. Let each line land. This is where people nod and feel seen.

The Flip: Speed up slightly. Energy shift. You're giving them hope now.

CTA: Direct eye contact. Confident. "I've been there. I got out. You can too."
