Here's how to answer ANY interview question with the same 15 stories.

"Tell me about pressure."

That's 3 different stories, right? Wrong.

[10-25 sec - CONCEPT]

Leadership. Teamwork. Problem-solving. Communication. Conflict. Failure. Initiative.

"Which of my 15 stories best fits this question?"

That's how you walk into an interview with total confidence. You've already matched every possible question to a proven answer.

Performance Notes

Hook: Say the example questions in different "interviewer" voices. Make it feel like rapid fire questions coming at you.

Concept: Slow down on "It depends on how you FRAME it." That's the key insight. Let it land.

Payoff: The "picking from a menu" line should feel relieving. Smile when you say it. This is the "aha" moment.

CTA: "Most people wing it. Engineers systematize it." — Pause between sentences. This is your tagline.
