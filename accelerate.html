How to Get Promoted in the AI Era

The Leadership Question

"If AI can do the technical work, what makes me promotable?"

Answer: The skills AI can't replace are exactly what gets you promoted:

  • Strategic thinking: AI executes plans; you create them
  • Cross-functional communication: AI can't align stakeholders
  • Mentoring: AI can't coach your junior engineers
