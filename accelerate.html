Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I'm ready for promotion?

If you're consistently delivering strong results but not getting promoted, you're likely missing visibility, positioning, or strategic relationships‚Äînot performance. That's exactly what I help with.

Many don't! We'll work to understand informal promotion criteria, build relationships with decision-makers, and create your own path forward.

Can you guarantee I'll get promoted?

I can't control your company's decisions, but I can give you the exact frameworks and strategies that drove my 4 promotions. You'll have a clear action plan and know exactly what to execute.

What if I'm great technically but struggle with visibility?

Perfect! That's the most common gap. I'll show you how to communicate impact, build relationships, and get noticed without feeling inauthentic or "salesy."

How is this different from my manager's mentorship?

Your manager is invested in your current role. I provide an outside perspective, industry best practices, and frameworks from someone who's navigated rapid promotion cycles successfully.

What if I want to move into management but have no experience?

I made that exact transition! We'll develop your leadership skills through IC work, find opportunities to demonstrate leadership, and position you for your first official role.
