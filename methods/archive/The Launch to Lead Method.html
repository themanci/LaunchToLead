The Launch to Lead Method: Curriculum Overview

Track 1: LAUNCH (The Entry)

Target: Students & Juniors.
Promise: "Land the John Deere Job."
  • Phase 1: The Me-in-We Extraction.
    (Identifying individual contribution).
  • Phase 2: The Impact Equation.
    (Task + Metric + How + Why).
  • Phase 3: The Iceberg Protocol.
    (The Master Manuscript & The Binder Strategy).
  • Phase 4: The High-Contrast Interview.
    (The "Paint the Pain" Narrative).

Track 2: PIVOT (The Transition)

Target: Stuck Professionals / Domain Switchers.
Promise: "Change Careers Without Quitting."
  • Phase 1: The Trojan Horse.
    (Solving old problems with new tools).
  • Phase 2: The 10% Margin.
    (Funding the pivot with efficiency arbitrage).
  • Phase 3: The Shadow Campaign.
    (Bypassing HR via the Vouch Vector).
  • Phase 4: The Necessity Anchor.
    (Using "Deliverable Panic" to force learning).

Track 3: ACCELERATE (The Ascent)

Target: Senior Engineers / Aspiring Managers.
Promise: "Get Promoted in Record Time."
  • Phase 1: The Visibility Pyramid.
    (Performance -> Awareness -> Relationship -> Opportunity).
  • Phase 2: The 85% Rule.
    (The Mastery Threshold & The Service-Based Ask).
  • Phase 3: Strategic Expansion.
    (The "Not My Job" Funeral).
  • Phase 4: The Reference Nudge.
    (The Reputation Wildfire).
