Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical pivot take?

6-18 months depending on the disciplines and your starting point. My mechanical-to-software pivot took about 2 years for a full role change, but I was contributing to software projects within 6 months.

Do I need to leave my company to pivot?

Not necessarily! Internal pivots are often easier because you have credibility and relationships. I'll help you navigate internal transfers strategically.

What if I don't have coding experience?

That's exactly where I started! I'll recommend the right learning path based on your target role and provide realistic timelines.

Can I pivot while working full-time?

Absolutely. That's how I did it. We'll create a realistic plan that fits your schedule‚Äîusually 5-10 hours per week of skill building.

What if my company doesn't have openings in my target discipline?

We'll develop a strategy for external opportunities while building skills internally. Your current role can still provide relevant project experience.

Will my mechanical background hurt me in software roles?

The opposite! Cross-functional engineers are incredibly valuable. I'll show you how to pitch your unique perspective as a competitive advantage.
