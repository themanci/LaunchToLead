Google Forms Setup Guide

Step-by-step instructions for two university club partnership intake forms

Form A: GOECE Graduate Organization of Electrical and Computer Engineering — includes resume upload (first 10 get free audit)

Form B: GradSWE Graduate Society of Women Engineers — no resume upload (president collects resumes manually and sends to you)

FORM A GOECE — Resume Audit + Session Registration

Step-by-step creation

1

Go to forms.google.com → click Blank form (or the + button)

2

Form title:

Free Resume Audit + Live Workshop — GOECE
3

Form description:

I'm partnering with GOECE to offer 10 free resume audits to club members — first come, first served. Submit your resume and info below. The first 10 complete submissions will receive a full written audit returned via email. Everyone who fills out this form (audited or not) is invited to the 1-hour live group session where I'll walk through the biggest issues I found across all resumes (anonymized) and open the floor for Q&A. Even if you're not in the first 10, showing up to the live session means you'll hear real feedback on real resumes — that alone is worth your time. PDF resumes only. One submission per person.
4

Now add each question below. For each one: click the + (add question) button, set the question type, paste the title and description, and toggle Required on/off as noted.

Question 10: Upload Your Resume (PDF only) Required
File upload
Important: When you select "File upload" as the question type, Google will ask you to confirm that uploaded files will be stored in your Google Drive. Click Continue.

Settings for the file upload question:
• Check Allow only specific file types → select PDF only
• Maximum number of files: 1
• Maximum file size: 10 MB

Toggle Required ON.

Form settings (gear icon ⚙️ at top)

  • Responses tab: Turn ON Collect email addresses (adds a verified email field at the top — acts as a backup to your Question 2, and lets Google notify you of new responses)
  • Responses tab: Turn ON Limit to 1 response (prevents duplicate submissions)
  • Presentation tab: Turn OFF Show link to submit another response
  • Presentation tab → Confirmation message: Paste the text below
Thanks for submitting! Here's what happens next: • If you're in the first 10, you'll receive your written resume audit via email within 3–5 business days. • Everyone who submitted will be invited to the 1-hour live group session — even if you weren't in the first 10, you'll benefit from hearing the feedback and Q&A. • Keep an eye on your email for the Zoom link and schedule. Questions? Reply to the email you receive or reach out to your club leadership.

After creating: link the responses to a spreadsheet

1

Click the Responses tab at the top of your form

2

Click the green Sheets icon (Link to Sheets)

3

Select Create a new spreadsheet → name it GOECE Resume Audit Submissions

4

Responses will auto-populate into the sheet. When you hit 10 valid entries, go back to the form and toggle Accepting responses OFF (top of Responses tab).

Quick Tips

  • Customize the theme: Click the palette icon (🎨) at top → pick a dark header color or upload your own banner image to look more polished.
  • Preview before sharing: Click the eye icon (👁) at top-right to see what students will see.
  • Get notifications: In the Responses tab, click the three-dot menu → Get email notifications for new responses. This way you know the moment someone submits.
  • Shorten the link: When you click Send, you can check Shorten URL to get a cleaner link for the club leader to share.
  • Duplicate for future clubs: Once Form A is built, you can duplicate it (three-dot menu on Google Forms homepage → Make a copy) and just change the club name in the title and description. Same for Form B.
