Murilo B. - From Stuck to Strategically Positioned

Accelerate Success at John Deere

The Challenge

"Before we started working together, my biggest challenge was feeling stuck in my career development. I had goals and ambitions, but I lacked clarity about the best path forward and the strategies needed to progress with confidence. I also struggled to translate my potential into visible results and didn't have a structured plan to improve my performance and positioning."

Murilo was a senior engineer at John Deere with strong technical knowledge and drive, but he was struggling to position himself for promotion and transition deeper into software. He had the ambition to move from design into the software area but lacked technical direction and confidence to make that change effectively. This created a sense of stagnation—he knew he had potential to contribute more strategically but wasn't sure how to bridge the gap between where he was and where he wanted to be.

The Breakthrough

"Instead of treating the shift as a leap into the unknown, you helped me break it down into practical steps: identifying the skills I already had that were transferable, mapping the new capabilities I needed to develop, and creating a realistic learning roadmap. This framework gave me clarity, direction, and confidence, and made the transition feel achievable rather than overwhelming."

Through our Accelerate coaching, Murilo learned to build visibility, cultivate strategic relationships that would pay off in 6-12 months, and leverage the Trojan Horse strategy. He convinced his manager to assign him a technician to direct, giving him real management experience and proof of leadership capability before getting the official promotion. This created undeniable evidence he was ready for the next level.

What Made the Difference:

  • • Personalized, Not Generic: "Your mentoring style stood out because it wasn't generic or theoretical—it was personalized, practical, and grounded in real-world experience. You took the time to understand my goals, strengths, and challenges, and then tailored the guidance to my specific situation."
  • • Practical Frameworks: Breaking down complex transitions into actionable steps with clear milestones
  • • AI Mastery: "Your technique on how to use AI the right way has been a game changer for me. Learning how to craft effective prompts and leverage AI thoughtfully has not only improved my technical results but also strengthened my communication, decision-making, and overall professional performance."

The Result

Accelerated software transition + leadership proof before promotion

Murilo's transformation after implementing Accelerate strategies:
