A/B Testing Recommendations

Test 1: Design 3 (Lime - brand consistency) vs Design 4 (Orange - urgency) for homepage popup

Test 2: Design 2 (Blue/Purple - premium) for LinkedIn cold traffic (tech crowd)

Test 3: Design 5 (Navy/Cyan - professional) for blog visitors (intent-driven organic traffic)

Test 4: Design 1 (Emerald - trust) for email list warm audience

Winner Hypothesis: Design 3 or 4 will likely win for cold traffic (high contrast + urgency), Design 5 for warm traffic (professional credibility)
