AJ M. - From Overwhelmed to Promoted in 18 Months

Accelerate Success at John Deere

The Challenge

"Before we met, my position faced many challenges. The #1 challenge in my mind was the lack of a clear and defined processes for accomplishing my work."
"The challenge created chaos not just for my specific role but for my whole team. It was very frustrating coming to work not knowing if anything had changed since yesterday or if plans were going to change today. It made me reconsider if I wanted to stay in my role."

AJ was an entry-level engineer at John Deere struggling with overwhelming chaos and lack of structure. He wasn't set up for success with his workload, and the constant uncertainty was pushing him to question whether he even wanted to stay. He needed to develop ownership, accountability, and the skills to rise above the noise.

The Breakthrough

"The most valuable piece of advice you gave me is to advocate for myself and properly show what I have accomplished. Early on in my career I had my nose down, grinding away at work and projects but once goals were met and accomplished I simply moved on to the next without showing what I had accomplished."

Through our Accelerate coaching, AJ learned that hard work alone wasn't enough—he needed to make his impact visible. He used the Trojan Horse method, stepping up to own an entire product line before being promoted. He learned to influence without authority, communicate effectively with skip-level managers, and bring forward ideas that earned recognition.

What Made the Difference:

"What sets you apart from others is your ability to try to look at things from multiple angles. You genuinely take the time to look at an issue or task from many angles and try to get a good representation of how everyone sees it, not just how you see it. That has allowed you to provide better, more honest guidance and assistance."
  • • Self-Advocacy: Learned to showcase accomplishments instead of just grinding in silence
  • • Trojan Horse Method: Took on an entire product line before being promoted
  • • Skip-Level Visibility: Built relationships with managers above his direct supervisor
  • • Multi-Angle Problem Solving: Developed ability to see situations from multiple perspectives

The Result

Promoted from Entry Level to Engineer II in ~18 Months

"I saw an increase in efficiency in my work, an increase of accreditation by showing my work, and an overall higher job satisfaction."
"Working with you definitely helped my career trajectory as it has already impacted me as I am now an Engineer II and doing well. I believe I am set up to continue to move upwards as well."
  • ✓ Career Advancement: Promoted to Engineer II in approximately 18 months from when coaching began
  • ✓ Increased Efficiency: Major improvement in work productivity and output
  • ✓ Recognition & Accreditation: Learned to properly showcase accomplishments and gain visibility
  • ✓ Higher Job Satisfaction: Transformed from reconsidering leaving to thriving in his role
  • ✓ Positioned for Continued Growth: Set up to continue moving upward in his career
"He does not rush to judgement as he takes his time properly identifying the surface as well as underlying issues of any situation. He creates detailed plans and goals to attack those problems. Most importantly, he gives fair and honest feedback and advice on how to solve a problem or achieve a goal."
