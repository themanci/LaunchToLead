Implementation Recommendations

A/B Testing Strategy

  • Test 1: Design 1 (Resume) vs Design 3 (Black Hole) — both target callback problem
  • Test 2: Design 2 (Interview) vs Design 4 (21-Day) — interview-ready angle vs complete roadmap
  • Test 3: Design 5 (ATS) for technical traffic sources (GitHub, Stack Overflow ads)

Traffic Source Matching

  • LinkedIn cold traffic: Design 1 or 3 (resume transformation proof)
  • Blog visitors: Design 4 (21-day roadmap = content upgrade)
  • Email list: Design 2 (interview binder = new offer for warm audience)
  • SEO organic: Design 5 (ATS-focused matches search intent)

Conversion Optimization

  • → Keep popup on 3-5 second delay (not exit intent)
