Hormozi Principles for All Videos

Value Equation

Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood) ÷ (Time Delay × Effort Required)

Core Messaging Pillars

  • 1. Pain-First: Always lead with the problem
  • 2. Contrarian Truth: Challenge "spray and pray"
  • 3. Specificity: Named methods, exact numbers
  • 4. Credibility Stack: Engineer, not recruiter
  • 5. Risk Reversal: The Launch Blueprint Guarantee™

Voice & Tone

  • Direct, no fluff
  • "I'm going to be honest with you..."
  • Numbers and specifics over vague promises
  • Conversational, not salesy
  • Slight urgency without desperation

Production Notes

Recording Setup

  • Camera: Eye-level, slight downward angle
  • Lighting: Ring light or three-point lighting
  • Background: Clean, professional (bookshelf/office)
  • Audio: External mic (lapel/shotgun) - CRITICAL
  • Framing: Head and shoulders, slight headroom

Editing Style

  • Quick cuts (no long pauses)
  • Text overlays for key stats/names
  • B-roll to break up talking head
  • Captions/subtitles for accessibility
  • Music bed (subtle, not distracting)
