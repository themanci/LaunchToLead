## Hormozi Principles for All Videos

### Value Equation (Reference in Every Script)
$$\text{Value} = \frac{\text{Dream Outcome} \times \text{Perceived Likelihood}}{\text{Time Delay} \times \text{Effort Required}}$$

### Core Messaging Pillars
1. **Pain-First** - Always lead with the problem
2. **Contrarian Truth** - Challenge the "spray and pray" advice
3. **Specificity** - Named methods, exact numbers, concrete results
4. **Credibility Stack** - Engineer, not recruiter. Hired engineers. 7 years at Deere.
5. **Risk Reversal** - The Launch Blueprint Guaranteeâ„¢

### Voice & Tone
- Direct, no fluff
- "I'm going to be honest with you..."
- Numbers and specifics over vague promises
- Conversational, not salesy
- Slight urgency without desperation

# Production Notes

## General Guidelines

### Length Targets
| Video | Target Length | Max Length |
|-------|--------------|------------|
| Homepage (index.html) | 60 seconds | 90 seconds |
| Launch Page (launch.html) | 90 seconds | 120 seconds |
| Packages Page (packages.html) | 2 minutes | 3 minutes |
| About Page (about.html) | 3 minutes | 4 minutes |

### Recording Setup
- **Camera:** Eye-level, slight downward angle (power position)
- **Lighting:** Ring light or three-point lighting
- **Background:** Clean, professional (bookshelf, office, or solid color)
- **Audio:** External mic (lapel or shotgun) - audio quality matters more than video
- **Framing:** Head and shoulders, slight headroom

### Editing Style
- Quick cuts (no long pauses)
- Text overlays for key stats/names
- B-roll to break up talking head
- Captions/subtitles for accessibility
- Music bed (subtle, not distracting)

### Thumbnail Suggestions
| Video | Thumbnail Text | Expression |
|-------|---------------|------------|
| Homepage | "Why 250 Apps = 0 Jobs" | Serious, direct |
| Launch Page | "The $40K Mistake" | Concerned, intense |
| Packages Page | "21 Days to Hired" | Confident, professional |
| About Page | "My Story" | Warm, approachable |

---

## Recording Priority Order

1. **launch.html** (highest intent page - visitors are already interested)
