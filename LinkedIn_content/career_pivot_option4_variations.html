Variation Summary

Variation 1: Dark emerald-950 background with logo header, high contrast white text, lime-300 accents

Variation 2: Light slate-50 background, strong emerald-900 accents, colorful left borders, prominent logo banner

Variation 3: Emerald-to-teal gradient, large watermark logo, lime-400 number badges, glassmorphism cards

Variation 4: Split design with branded banner top, gradient cards in emerald/teal/lime progression

Variation 5: High contrast white background, bold 4px emerald-950 borders, lime-400 accents, corner logo badge

Color Palette Used:

  • Emerald: emerald-950, emerald-900, emerald-800 (dark backgrounds)
  • Teal: teal-900, teal-800, teal-700 (gradient transitions)
  • Lime: lime-400, lime-600 (high contrast accents)
  • Slate: slate-50 (light backgrounds)
  • White with opacity for glassmorphism effects

All variations include: LaunchToLead logo, "The Pivot Method" branding, website URL, and maintain 3-step structure
