The Last 5 Minutes
3 questions that actually impress interviewers
1
"What do most people coming into this role with a background like mine struggle with most in the first year?"
Why it works:
Shows self-awareness and desire to overcome challenges proactively.
2
"What are the biggest challenges this role will face in the next 1-2 years?"
Why it works:
Demonstrates strategic thinking and long-term perspective on the role.
3
"What gaps in technical knowledge or tools knowledge do you feel like your team has now that I might be able to fill with my experiences we've talked about?"
Why it works:
Shows value-add mindset and ties your skills directly to team needs.
