Stop treating interviews like a one-way street.

You're not just there to prove you're a fit for THEM.

They need to prove they're a fit for YOU.

I've seen too many engineers accept offers just because the company has a "good reputation" or a recognizable name.

6 months later? They're miserable.

Before you say yes, ask yourself:

  • What challenges is the team solving right now?
  • How does this role contribute to the bigger picture?
  • How has the team grown or changed recently?
  • How do you celebrate wins on the team?
  • What are the biggest gaps in knowledge or tools?

A "good company" doesn't guarantee a good job.
