The secret to a great follow-up is capturing details while they're fresh. After each conversation, take 30 seconds to jot notes on your phone.

If You Don't Get a Response

Wait 5-7 Days

Give them time. Recruiters are busy after career fairs. If you haven't heard back, send a brief follow-up:

"Hi Sarah, just wanted to follow up on my email from last week. I'm still very interested in the Product Development rotation and wanted to confirm you received my application. Happy to provide any additional information. Thanks again!"

No Response = No Rejection

Silence doesn't mean they're not interested. Your application might still be in process. Keep applying to other companies while you wait.

Connect on LinkedIn

If email doesn't work, try connecting on LinkedIn with a personalized note. Some recruiters check LinkedIn more than email.
