Career Fair Strategy Tips

1

Warm up with a backup company first

Don't start with your dream company. Get comfortable, shake out nerves.

2

Hit your top 5 companies in the first hour

Lines get longer. Recruiters get tired. Strike early.

3

Take notes AFTER each conversation

Step away, jot down name + key points + follow-up actions.

4

Ask for contact info

"Can I follow up with you by email?" Get a business card or email.

5

Know when to walk away

If the line is 20+ deep, move on and come back later (or after the rush).
