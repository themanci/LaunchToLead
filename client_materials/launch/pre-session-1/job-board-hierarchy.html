The Concept

What is ROTI?

Return on Time Invested

Time spent per quality lead. The goal is to maximize your response rate while minimizing wasted effort.

‚úÖ High ROTI (Top Tiers)

30 min on Tier 1 (research + tailored app)

= 15% response rate

‚ùå Low ROTI (Bottom Tiers)

5 min on Tier 10 (Easy Apply click)

= <1% response rate

The truth: Students prioritize convenience (Easy Apply) over probability. That's why they get ghosted.

Tactics

The Tactical Playbook

The 24-Hour Rule

Only apply to jobs posted in the last 24-48 hours. Older postings are likely filled or ghost jobs.

üîç

The Boolean Hack

Use advanced search operators to filter noise:

("embedded" OR "firmware") AND NOT "senior"

"new grad" OR "entry level" OR "0-2 years"

‚úåÔ∏è

The Double Tap

Apply online, then DM a recruiter or peer at the company. Never rely on the portal alone.
