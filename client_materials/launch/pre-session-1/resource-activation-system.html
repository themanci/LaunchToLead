Professors

üé∞

The Adjunct Jackpot

If they're industry pros teaching part-time, they are dual-hatted.

They have direct hiring connections at their "day job" company‚Äîand they're looking for exactly the talent they're training in class.

‚è∞

Timing Matters

Office hours mid-semester, not finals week. Professors are overwhelmed at semester end.

‚úÖ Weeks 4-10
‚ùå Finals week
üíé

Bring Value First

Don't show up empty-handed. Reference their research or a class project.

Example: "I read your paper on [Topic] and it connects to our class project on [X]. I'm curious how this applies in industry."

The Ask Script

"I'm really interested in [Field]. Do you know anyone working in this space I could talk to?"

This gives them an easy way to help without putting them on the spot for a job.

üì¨ Follow-Up Protocol

If they make an intro, close the loop:

"Thanks for the intro to [Person]; here's what I learned from our chat: [Brief insight]. Really appreciate you connecting us!"

This makes them feel valued and more likely to help again.
