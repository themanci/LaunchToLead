üîë The "Next Action Date" Rule

The most important column in your spreadsheet. Every active row must have a date for the next step.

  • ‚Ä¢ If the date passes with no action, the row turns red
  • ‚Ä¢ Set default follow-up: 7 days post-apply
  • ‚Ä¢ Add a "Stuck Alert" column for rows over 14 days with no movement

Why? Ignored follow-ups extend job searches by 20+ days.

üìù The Close-Out Rule

After 2 weeks of silence, mark as Ghosted. Send one final professional nudge, then archive with a "Lessons Learned" note (e.g., "Skills gap?" "Wrong job board tier?"). Prevents dwelling and frees mental slots for new prospects.
