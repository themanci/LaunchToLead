THE 4 REFRAMES

Rewire Your Thinking

1

Seek Advice, Not Favors

‚ùå Old Mindset (Taker)

"Please give me a job."

‚úÖ New Mindset (Learner)

"I value your perspective on [Topic]."

Why It Works: People love to give advice. It strokes their ego and feels low-risk.

Example: Instead of "Refer me?", say "What's one skill that helped you early at [Company]?"

2

The Permission Script

‚ùå Old Mindset

"I'm bothering them."

‚úÖ New Mindset

The Reality: Most seniors want to help the next generation. You're giving them an easy way to do good.

The "Fellow Alum" Angle

‚ùå Old Mindset

4

‚ùå Old Mindset
