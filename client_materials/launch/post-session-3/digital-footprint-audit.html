LinkedIn Deep Dive

LinkedIn is the #1 platform recruiters check. Make it work for you:

Professional headshot

Clear face, good lighting, appropriate attire. No selfies, party pics, or pets.

Compelling headline

Not just your job title. Include value proposition or target role.

Custom banner image

Professional, relevant to your field. Not the default LinkedIn blue.

About section tells your story

First person, engaging, shows personality. Includes what you're looking for.

Experience has accomplishments, not just duties

Use the CAR format. Quantify results where possible.

Skills section is complete and relevant

Top 3 skills should match your target role. Remove outdated skills.

Custom URL

linkedin.com/in/yourname not linkedin.com/in/firstname-lastname-123456789

"Open to Work" settings configured

Set to recruiters only if you don't want current employer to see.
