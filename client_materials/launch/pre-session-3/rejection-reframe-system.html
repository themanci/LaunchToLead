The Debugging Approach

When you track your data, you can identify where the bottleneck is and fix it:

Low Application ‚Üí Response Rate (<5%)

Problem: Your resume or applications aren't getting through.

Fix: Improve resume bullets, add keywords, tailor to each role, apply to more relevant positions.

Low Phone Screen ‚Üí Interview Rate (<40%)

Problem: You're not converting recruiter calls.

Fix: Practice your pitch, prepare company research, improve phone presence, ask better questions.

Low Interview ‚Üí Offer Rate (<10%)

Problem: You're interviewing but not closing.

Fix: More mock interviews, better stories, stronger technical prep, work on presence and rapport.

The Rejection Journal

After each rejection, log what you learned. Turn pain into data.

Questions to Ask Yourself:

1. At what stage did I get rejected?

Application, phone screen, interview, final round?

2. What went well?

3. What could I have done better?

Be specific and actionable.

4. Did I get any feedback?

If not, can I request it?

5. What will I do differently next time?

One concrete change.
