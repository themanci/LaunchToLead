4 Hacks to Find Great Companies

1

The Supplier Hack

Don't apply to Ford‚Äîapply to who makes their components:

Seats: Adient, Lear
Tires: Michelin, Bridgestone

üí° Use Crunchbase or LinkedIn to map supply chains (search "Tesla suppliers 2025")

2

The Map Hack

Search "Industrial Park [Your City]" on Google Maps. You'll find 50-person firms with senior engineers retiring and zero LinkedIn presence.

üí° These companies are desperate and will train you. Low competition, high mentorship.

3

The Funding Hack

Check Crunchbase for Series B/C companies. They have money to hire but aren't famous yet.

üí° Recent funding = urgent hiring needs. Less competition than established names.

4

The Acquisition Hack

Recently acquired startups need to backfill talent. Search "[Company] acquisition 2025" and apply to the acquirer's integration teams.

üí° Acquisitions create hiring urgency and new team formation‚Äîperfect for new grads.
