Mohamed I. - From Contract to Permanent to Software

Launch Success at John Deere

The Challenge

"Struggling with interviews."

Mohamed was an entry-level contract engineer at John Deere who struggled to convert interviews into job offers. His interview performance was limiting his potential opportunities, keeping him in contract roles when he wanted permanent employment and a path to software engineering.

The Breakthrough

"The most valuable advice was to have a mock interview."

Through dedicated mock interview sessions and personalized feedback, Mohamed developed the skills and confidence to succeed in real interviews. Our coaching covered technical interview preparation, behavioral question frameworks, and strategies for showcasing his potential. Over the years of working together, we tackled interview preparation, software skill development, and strategic career positioning.

The Result

Landed permanent salaried engineering role, then pivoted from mechanical CI to software

Mohamed transformed his career trajectory in three major steps:

  • Step 1: Converted from contract to permanent salaried engineering role at John Deere
  • Step 2: Successfully pivoted from mechanical continuous improvement to software engineering
  • Step 3: Developed competitive software skills to thrive in the corporate landscape
