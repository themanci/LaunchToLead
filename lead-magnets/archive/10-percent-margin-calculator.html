The Strategy

The 10% Margin Rule

10% of 40 hours = 4 hours/week

That's 16 hours/month. 200 hours/year.

Enough to build a career-changing portfolio.

The Problem Most People Face:

❌ "I'll work on my pivot after hours" → Burnout in 2 weeks

❌ "I'll do it on weekends" → Family/life guilt kills momentum

❌ "I need to quit to focus" → Financial stress derails everything

The 10% Solution:

Reclaim wasted time during work hours and redirect it toward pivot-building activities.

✓ No guilt: You're at work, getting paid
✓ No burnout: Evenings and weekends stay free
✓ No risk: You're building proof while employed

4 hours/week for 6 months = 100 hours of portfolio work

Action Plan

How to Reclaim Your 10%

1. Efficiency Arbitrage

Get faster at current work → Reinvest saved time in pivot work

Examples:

  • • Automate repetitive tasks (scripts, macros, tools)
  • • Use AI assistants for drafting/research
  • • Batch similar tasks instead of context switching
  • • Template common communications

2. Strategic Delegation

What can you hand off, automate, or stop doing entirely?

Questions to ask:

  • • What work doesn't actually require my expertise?
  • • What meetings can I skip or send notes to instead?
  • • What "nice-to-have" tasks can I drop without consequences?

3. Protected Time Blocks

Schedule your 10% like a recurring meeting—it's non-negotiable

Sample schedule:

  • • Mon/Wed: 8-9:30am → Pivot work (3 hours)
  • • Friday: 2-3pm → Reflection & planning (1 hour)
  • • Total: 4 hours/week, carved into existing work time

The Permission Paradox

Most people wait for permission to invest in their growth. Don't. If you're delivering on your core responsibilities, how you structure the rest of your time is up to you. Reclaim it.
