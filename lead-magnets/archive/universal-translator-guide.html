The Solution

The Universal Translator

A 3-step process to make any technical achievement instantly understandable

1

What (Technical)

State the technical action in plain terms

Template: "I [action] using [technology]"

Example: "I automated deployment using CI/CD pipelines"

2

So What (Impact)

Explain the measurable outcome

Template: "Which [metric] by [number]"

Example: "Which reduced deployment time from 4 hours to 15 minutes"

3

Now What (Business Value)

Connect to business outcomes anyone can understand

Template: "Allowing [business outcome]"

Example: "Allowing the team to ship features 10x faster and respond to customer needs same-day"

The Magic: Non-technical people understand Step 2 & 3. Technical people respect Step 1. Everyone wins.
