Before You Reach Out

What should I prepare for the discovery call?

Nothing formal! Just think about your current situation, career goals, and biggest challenges. We'll discuss how coaching can help.

How quickly will I hear back?

I typically respond within 24 hours on weekdays. If it's urgent, mention that in your message.

Do you offer free sessions?

The 15-minute discovery call is free to ensure we're a good fit. After that, all coaching sessions are paid packages.

What if I'm not sure which service I need?

That's totally fine! The discovery call is perfect for figuring out which track (Launch, Pivot, or Accelerate) fits your goals best.
