## Risk Reversal

Two separate guarantees:

**Guarantee 1 — After Session 1**
If you're not happy and don't feel it's valuable, we both walk away. No further commitment, no questions asked, no feelings hurt, still friends. You will at minimum walk away with a powerful resume to start landing interviews.

**Guarantee 2 — Complete the Full Month**
Complete all 4 sessions, do the homework, and submit 50+ job applications. If you do, you are guaranteed to walk away with:
- A fully rebuilt resume that shows value and impact
- A story vault of documented interview examples with competency mapping complete
- In-depth mock interview practice with detailed feedback (recording, transcript, and notes)

If we complete the month and those three deliverables are not in your hands, I continue working with you at no additional charge until they are.

## The Plan

### Core Offer
Land a top-tier engineering role.

---

### The 3 Problems Blocking That Outcome

1. Not enough interviews
2. Using bad interview examples
3. Bad interview performance

---

### What Success Looks Like (One Per Problem)

1. Landing more interviews via a stronger resume
2. Using genuinely strong examples and experiences in interviews
3. Knocking interviews out of the park

---

### What Has to Be Fixed — 3 Levers Per Success Area

**1. Land more interviews with the resume**
- a) Fix the resume to show value and impact *(Impact Bullet Builder™, Me-in-We Extractor™, Hidden Metrics Finder™)*
- b) Improve application strategy *(Job Sourcing Strategy™, Application Pipeline™)*
- c) Learn how to network effectively *(Warm Intro Protocol™, Referral Request Script™)*

**2. Use really good examples and experiences in interviews**
- a) Understand what makes an example genuinely strong *(High-Impact Story Framework™, 3-Tier Impact Ladder™)*
- b) Understand behavioral interview competencies and how to map personal experiences to them *(Competency Story Map™)*
- c) Document examples in detail and memorize them before the interview *(Master Story Vault™, Interview Cheat Sheet™)*

**3. Knock interviews out of the park**
- a) Build a detailed interview strategy *(Power Pause + Story Arsenal™, Numbered Roadmap™)*
- b) Practice through mock interviews with structured feedback *(Daily Story Practice™)*
- c) Execute a post-interview strategy and negotiate the offer *(Research Your Worth System™, Entry-Level Negotiation Scripts™)*

## Sales Funnel

All content — LinkedIn posts, short-form, long-form, email, everything — drives to two places:

1. **Directly to the full offer doc** (primary destination)
2. **CTA to comment or reply** → triggers delivery of the micro lead magnet → micro lead magnet also routes to the full offer doc

### Micro Lead Magnet Specs
- A custom AI tool that completely solves one small, specific problem
- Fully automated delivery
- Auto-routes recipient to the full offer doc after delivery
- Rotates monthly (new tool each month)
