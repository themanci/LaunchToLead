Navigating the AI-Era Job Market

The Reality: Entry-level hiring is down. Companies are using AI for tasks junior engineers used to do.

But Here's What They're Not Telling You: Companies still need engineers who can:

  • Prompt and validate AI outputs (AI writes code, but someone needs to verify it works)
  • Debug AI-generated solutions (AI makes mistakes‚Äîa lot)
  • Design systems (AI can't architect; it can only execute what you tell it)
  • Communicate with non-technical stakeholders (AI definitely can't do this)

What This Means for You:

  • Your resume needs to show you can work WITH AI, not compete against it
  • Highlight projects where you used ChatGPT, GitHub Copilot, or other AI tools effectively
  • Frame yourself as "AI-augmented engineer" not "entry-level engineer"

I'll help you:

  • Reposition your experience to highlight AI collaboration
  • Build portfolio projects that demonstrate AI tool proficiency
  • Navigate the "AI paradox": companies want AI-savvy engineers but are hiring fewer entry-level roles
