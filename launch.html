Frequently Asked Questions

What majors do you work with?

All engineering majors! Mechanical, Electrical, Computer, Civil, Chemical, Aerospace, etc. I have personal experience pivoting from Mechanical to Software, so I understand diverse engineering backgrounds.

Not at all! While I can help with FAANG prep, my specialty is large traditional engineering companies (automotive, aerospace, ag-tech, manufacturing) that are becoming tech-forward. Think John Deere, Boeing, Caterpillar, GM, etc.

How far in advance should I start?

Ideally 3-6 months before career fairs or application deadlines. But I've helped students prep in 2 weeks before an interview. The sooner, the better!
