1. Service Description

Launch to Lead Engineering provides professional career coaching services for engineers and technology professionals. Our primary offering is:

  • The Launch Path: Career coaching for college students and early-career engineers (resume development, interview preparation, job search strategy). Includes 3 coaching sessions delivered over 21 days.

Future Offerings: Additional coaching tracks may be offered in the future. These will be announced on our website when available.

All coaching sessions are 90 minutes long, delivered via video conference (Zoom or Google Meet).

2. Payment Terms

Pricing: Current pricing is discussed during your free strategy session. We offer flexible payment options including pay-in-full and installment plans.

Payment Methods: We accept Stripe (credit/debit cards), Zelle, or Venmo.

Strategy Session: Before purchasing any package, you'll have a free 15-minute strategy session to discuss your goals and answer any questions about pricing and payment.

Client Cancellations: You may cancel or reschedule a session with at least 24 hours advance notice without penalty. Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice will be considered a completed session and no refund or rescheduling will be provided.

Coach Cancellations: If I need to cancel or reschedule due to illness, emergency, or other unforeseen circumstances, I'll provide as much notice as possible and work with you to reschedule at no additional cost.

I respect your privacy and will keep all information shared during our coaching sessions confidential. However, confidentiality has limits:

  • Information may be shared if required by law or legal process

Refund Policy

Clear expectations about payments and refunds

This Refund Policy outlines our approach to refunds, cancellations, and satisfaction commitments.

1. General Refund Policy

Services are non-refundable once delivered. This means:

  • Completed coaching sessions cannot be refunded
  • Payment for attended sessions is final
  • Materials and resources provided during sessions remain non-refundable

This policy protects both parties and ensures committed engagement in the coaching process.

2. The Launch Blueprint Guarantee™

We stand behind our coaching with The Launch Blueprint Guarantee™:

Complete the Launch Path program, do the work, and if you don't walk away with:

  • A completely transformed resume that actually sounds like you
  • 15+ powerful STAR examples ready to deploy in any interview
  • 10x more confidence in telling your career story

...we'll keep working with you at no extra charge until you do.

Requirements: To qualify for this guarantee, you must:

  • Attend all scheduled coaching sessions
  • Complete all assigned exercises and homework between sessions
  • Actively participate in the coaching process
  • Communicate any concerns before the final session

Your success is my priority. I only succeed when you succeed, and I'm dedicated to making sure you get real value from our work together.

3. First Session Evaluation

After your first coaching session:

  • If you feel the coaching isn't a good fit, please let me know immediately
  • We'll discuss your concerns and determine if adjustments can address them
  • If we mutually agree that coaching isn't the right solution, we'll work together to find an alternative approach
  • In exceptional circumstances where the coaching relationship clearly isn't working, we may offer a partial resolution on remaining sessions

Note: The first session itself is non-refundable, but we want to ensure you're comfortable continuing before moving forward with the rest of your package.

4. Unused Sessions

Package Not Started:

  • If you haven't attended any sessions yet, you may request a refund
  • A processing fee (10% or $50, whichever is less) may be deducted for administrative costs
  • Request must be made at least 48 hours before your first scheduled session

Package Partially Completed:

  • Completed sessions are non-refundable
  • Remaining unused sessions may be eligible for partial credit toward future services or adjusted packages
  • Contact us to discuss options if you need to discontinue coaching mid-package

5. Payment Plan Considerations

Split Payment Plans:

  • If you stop payment after the first installment, access to remaining sessions is suspended
  • The first payment covers completed sessions; no refund is provided
  • To resume, you must complete the remaining payment

Pay in Full Packages:

  • You've paid upfront for all sessions
  • Completed sessions are non-refundable
  • Contact us if circumstances change and you need to pause or adjust your package

6. Force Majeure & Exceptional Circumstances

If unforeseen circumstances prevent us from delivering your coaching services:

  • Coach Unavailability: If I become unable to provide services due to illness, emergency, or other circumstances, you'll receive a refund for undelivered sessions
  • Technical Issues: If persistent technical problems prevent session delivery, we'll reschedule at no additional cost or provide alternative solutions
  • Major Life Events: If you experience significant life changes (medical emergency, relocation, etc.) that prevent participation, contact us to discuss options

7. No-Show & Cancellation Policy

As outlined in our Terms of Service:

  • Late Cancellations: Sessions cancelled with less than 24 hours notice are considered completed and non-refundable
  • No-Shows: Missed sessions without prior notice are counted as completed and non-refundable
  • Proper Cancellation: Cancel at least 24 hours in advance to reschedule without penalty

8. Dispute Resolution Process

If you have concerns about value, service quality, or billing:

  • Step 1: Contact us immediately at contact@launchtolead.io
  • Step 2: We'll schedule a call to discuss your concerns and explore solutions
  • Step 3: We'll work together in good faith to find a resolution that addresses your concerns
  • Step 4: If needed, we'll involve a neutral third party for mediation

Most concerns can be resolved through direct communication. We're committed to finding fair solutions.
