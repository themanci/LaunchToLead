Refund Policy

Clear expectations about payments and refunds

1. General Policy

Program sessions are non-refundable once delivered — completed sessions and delivered materials cannot be refunded.

2. The Guarantee

Give the program 60 days. If you show up, complete Missions 1–6, ask for help when you're stuck, and still don't have a clear Promotion-Ready Plan plus a stronger way to make your impact visible, we'll buy the program back from you at full price.

3. Payment Plans & Cancellations

If you're on an installment plan and stop payment, access to future sessions is paused until payment resumes. Sessions cancelled with less than 24 hours notice, or missed without notice, are treated as completed and non-refundable.

4. Questions or Disputes

Contact us first at contact@launchtolead.io before initiating a chargeback — we'll work in good faith to resolve any billing concern directly.
