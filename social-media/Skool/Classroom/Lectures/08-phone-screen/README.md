# Section 8: Phone Screen Lectures (3 Methods)

*Source: The Launch Method V3 - Section 8*

## Videos to Record

### 8.1 The Phone Screen Prep Guide™ ⭐ PRIORITY
- **Length:** 5-7 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Research the company and role before the call
  - Have your resume, the job posting, and notes ready
  - Prepare quiet, distraction-free environment
  - Have pen and paper for notes
  - Prepare your "Tell me about yourself" answer
  - Know your salary expectations range
  - Have your calendar ready for scheduling next steps

### 8.2 The Human Element (Phone Screen Edition)™
- **Length:** 5 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Build rapport even over phone
  - Smile while you talk (it comes through in your voice)
  - Show enthusiasm and energy
  - Listen carefully, don't interrupt
  - Use the recruiter's name naturally
  - Express genuine interest in the company/role

### 8.3 The Phone Screen Questions to Ask™
- **Length:** 5 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - "What are the next steps in the process?"
  - "What does success look like in this role?"
  - "Can you tell me more about the team I'd be working with?"
  - "What's the timeline for making a decision?"
  - Questions that show preparation without overstepping
  - Save detailed questions for later interview rounds
