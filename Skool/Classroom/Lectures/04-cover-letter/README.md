## Videos to Record

### 4.1 The Companion Cover Letter™
- **Length:** 8-10 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - When cover letters matter (and when they don't)
  - The "companion" approach: enhances resume, doesn't repeat it
  - Structure: Hook → Why This Company → Why You → Ask
  - Opening: Lead with specific company interest or connection
  - Body: 1-2 unique stories not fully captured in resume
  - Close: Clear call to action and enthusiasm
  - Keep to one page, 3-4 paragraphs max
  - Personalization signals genuine interest
