# Section 11: Special Circumstances Lectures (2 Methods)

*Source: The Launch Method V3 - Section 11*

## Videos to Record

### 11.1 The Gap Explanation Scripts™
- **Length:** 8-10 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Resume gaps are more common than you think
  - Honest, brief explanations work best
  - Focus on what you learned or did during the gap
  - Script examples for various gap situations:
    - Health/family: "I took time to address a personal matter, which is now resolved"
    - Job searching: "I was selective about finding the right fit"
    - Education/travel: Easy to frame positively
  - Don't over-explain or apologize
  - Pivot quickly to your qualifications

### 11.2 The Digital Footprint Audit™
- **Length:** 5-7 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Google yourself and see what employers see
  - Clean up social media (or set to private)
  - LinkedIn profile optimization
  - Professional email address
  - Remove or hide anything unprofessional
  - Build positive digital presence intentionally
  - Google alerts for your name
