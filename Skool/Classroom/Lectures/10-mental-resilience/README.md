# Section 10: Mental Resilience Lectures (5 Methods)

*Source: The Launch Method V3 - Section 10*

## Videos to Record

### 10.1 The Rejection Reframe System™ ⭐ PRIORITY
- **Length:** 8-10 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Rejection is redirection, not reflection of your worth
  - Each "no" gets you closer to the right "yes"
  - What you can learn from rejection (when feedback is available)
  - Maintaining momentum after setbacks
  - Separating your identity from application outcomes

### 10.2 The Burnout Prevention System™ ⭐ PRIORITY
- **Length:** 5-7 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Sustainable job search pacing
  - Set daily/weekly application limits
  - Schedule breaks and non-job-search activities
  - Recognize warning signs of burnout
  - Job searching is a marathon, not a sprint

### 10.3 The Ghosting Response Protocol™
- **Length:** 5-7 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Companies ghost - it's not personal
  - When to follow up (2-week rule)
  - Follow-up script that keeps doors open
  - When to move on mentally
  - Don't let ghosting derail your confidence

### 10.4 The Managing External Pressure Scripts™
- **Length:** 5-7 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Handling "Do you have a job yet?" questions
  - Scripts for family, friends, and well-meaning relatives
  - Setting boundaries around job search discussions
  - How to communicate your process without over-explaining
  - Maintaining relationships while job searching

### 10.5 The Comparison Trap Escape™
- **Length:** 5 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Stop comparing your timeline to others
  - LinkedIn makes everyone's path look smoother than it is
  - Your journey is unique - different isn't worse
  - Focus on your own progress, not others' highlights
  - Celebrate your wins, no matter how small
