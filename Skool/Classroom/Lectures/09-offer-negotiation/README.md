# Section 9: Offer & Negotiation Lectures (5 Methods)

*Source: The Launch Method V3 - Section 9*

## Videos to Record

### 9.1 The Research Your Worth System™ ⭐ PRIORITY
- **Length:** 8-10 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Use Glassdoor, Levels.fyi, Payscale for salary research
  - Factor in location, company size, industry
  - Understand total compensation (base, bonus, equity, benefits)
  - Know your range: target, minimum, ideal
  - Research what new grads actually make in your field

### 9.2 The Offer Comparison Tool™ ⭐ PRIORITY
- **Length:** 8-10 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Framework for comparing multiple offers
  - Beyond salary: benefits, PTO, growth opportunities
  - Normalize offers for location/cost of living
  - Weight factors based on your priorities
  - Long-term career trajectory considerations

### 9.3 The Entry-Level Negotiation Scripts™
- **Length:** 8-10 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Yes, new grads CAN negotiate (appropriately)
  - Script: "I'm very excited about this opportunity. Based on my research and the value I'll bring, I was hoping for [range]. Is there flexibility?"
  - Ask for what you want clearly and professionally
  - Negotiable beyond salary: start date, signing bonus, PTO, remote work
  - Be prepared for "no" and have a graceful response

### 9.4 The Offer Timeline Management Scripts™
- **Length:** 5-7 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - How to buy time when you need it
  - Script: "Thank you so much for this offer. I'm very excited. Can I have until [date] to give you my decision?"
  - Handling exploding offers
  - Communicating with other companies when you have an offer
  - Never ghost - maintain professional relationships

### 9.5 The First 90-Day Success Plan™
- **Length:** 5-7 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - How to start strong once you accept
  - First 30 days: Learn, listen, build relationships
  - Days 31-60: Start contributing, ask questions
  - Days 61-90: Take initiative, deliver results
  - Set yourself up for early success and growth
