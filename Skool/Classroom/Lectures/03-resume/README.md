# Section 3: Resume Lectures (9 Methods)

*Source: The Launch Method V3 - Section 3*

## Videos to Record

### 3.1 The Me-in-We Extractor™ ⭐ PRIORITY
- **Length:** 10-12 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Extract YOUR specific contributions from team projects
  - Identify your unique value-add in group work
  - Transition from "We did X" to "I contributed Y that resulted in Z"
  - Document individual ownership within collaborative efforts

### 3.2 The Impact Bullet Builder™ ⭐ PRIORITY
- **Length:** 8-10 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Formula: Action Verb + Task + Result/Impact
  - Quantify whenever possible (%, $, time saved, people impacted)
  - Focus on outcomes, not just activities
  - Examples of weak vs strong bullets

### 3.3 The Boring-to-Standout Upgrade™ ⭐ PRIORITY
- **Length:** 5-7 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Transform generic responsibilities into compelling achievements
  - Add specificity: tools, scale, complexity
  - Show impact and initiative
  - Before/after examples

### 3.4 The Universal Translator + ATS Pass™
- **Length:** 8-10 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Translate academic/project work into industry language
  - ATS (Applicant Tracking System) optimization basics
  - Keyword matching from job descriptions
  - Formatting for ATS readability

### 3.5 The Passion Proof Finder™
- **Length:** 5-7 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Identify experiences that demonstrate genuine interest in field
  - Side projects, coursework beyond requirements, volunteer work
  - Show intrinsic motivation beyond grades
  - Connect passion to career goals

### 3.6 The Hidden Metrics Finder™
- **Length:** 5-7 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Find quantifiable results you might have overlooked
  - Academic: class ranking, GPA context, project scope
  - Work: volume, efficiency, quality metrics
  - Activities: membership size, budget managed, events organized

### 3.7 The Domain Translator™
- **Length:** 5-7 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - For career changers and non-traditional backgrounds
  - Map transferable skills to target industry language
  - Highlight relevant overlap
  - Position diverse experience as an asset

### 3.8 The 4-Minute Tailored Bullet System™
- **Length:** 5-7 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Quick process to customize resume bullets per application
  - Identify 2-3 key requirements from job posting
  - Reorder and adjust bullets to highlight relevant experience
  - Maintain core resume while customizing strategically

### 3.9 The Asset Mindset Shift™
- **Length:** 5 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Reframe "I don't have enough experience" mindset
  - Recognize value in academic projects, internships, activities
  - Confidence in presenting what you have
  - Every experience has transferable value
