# Section 7: Career Fair Lectures (3 Methods)

*Source: The Launch Method V3 - Section 7*

## Videos to Record

### 7.1 The 60-90 Second Pitch Structure™ ⭐ PRIORITY
- **Length:** 8-10 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Concise elevator pitch for career fair conversations
  - Structure: Who you are → What you're studying/experienced in → What you're looking for → Why this company
  - Practice until natural, not memorized-sounding
  - Adapt based on company and role
  - Hook opener to stand out from other students
  - End with a question to engage the recruiter

### 7.2 The 24-48 Hour Follow-Up Protocol™
- **Length:** 5-7 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Send personalized follow-up within 24-48 hours
  - Reference specific conversation points
  - Template: Thank you + Something specific you discussed + Reiterate interest + Ask about next steps
  - LinkedIn connection request with personalized note
  - Keep brief but memorable

### 7.3 The Career Fair Prep Checklist™
- **Length:** 5-7 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Research attending companies beforehand
  - Prioritize target companies (have A, B, C tiers)
  - Prepare multiple copies of resume
  - Professional attire appropriate for industry
  - Bring notepad to take notes on conversations
  - Map out booth locations
  - Questions prepared for each target company
