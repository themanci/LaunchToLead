# Section 5: Interview Prep Lectures (12 Methods)

*Source: The Launch Method V3 - Section 5*

## Videos to Record

### 5.1 The Master Story Vault™ ⭐ PRIORITY
- **Length:** 10-12 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Create comprehensive collection of career stories
  - Categories: Leadership, Teamwork, Problem-Solving, Conflict, Initiative
  - Each story has: Situation, Task, Action, Result
  - 8-12 versatile stories that can be adapted to many questions

### 5.2 The Competency Story Map™ ⭐ PRIORITY
- **Length:** 8-10 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Map stories to common interview competencies
  - One story can cover multiple competencies
  - Ensure coverage of all major areas
  - Quick reference for interview prep

### 5.3 The Power Pause + Story Arsenal™
- **Length:** 5-7 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - It's okay to take a moment to think
  - "That's a great question, let me think..."
  - Mental retrieval of relevant story from vault
  - Collecting thoughts leads to better answers

### 5.4 The High-Impact Story Framework™ ⭐ PRIORITY
- **Length:** 8-10 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - STAR method: Situation, Task, Action, Result
  - Add "What I Learned" for extra depth
  - Emphasize YOUR specific actions
  - Quantify results when possible

### 5.5 The Numbered Roadmap™
- **Length:** 5-7 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Structure answers with clear organization
  - "There are three things I'd like to highlight..."
  - Helps interviewer follow along
  - Shows clear thinking and communication

### 5.6 The 3-Tier Impact Ladder™
- **Length:** 5-7 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Individual impact (what you did)
  - Team impact (how it helped colleagues)
  - Business/Organization impact (bottom line results)
  - Show you think beyond your own tasks

### 5.7 The Daily Story Practice™
- **Length:** 5 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Practice telling stories out loud
  - Time yourself (aim for 60-90 seconds per story)
  - Record and review for improvement
  - Build muscle memory for interview day

### 5.8 The Interview Cheat Sheet™
- **Length:** 5-7 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - One-page reference for interview day
  - Key stories, questions to ask, company research highlights
  - Salary expectations, your "why this company" answer
  - Keep accessible but don't read from it

### 5.9 The Visual Aid Binder™
- **Length:** 5-7 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Portfolio of work samples (where appropriate)
  - Project screenshots, designs, code samples
  - Use selectively to illustrate points
  - Especially valuable for creative/technical roles

### 5.10 The Human Element Questions™
- **Length:** 5-7 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Questions that show genuine interest in interviewer/team
  - "What do you enjoy most about working here?"
  - "What does success look like in this role?"
  - Build connection beyond transactional interview

### 5.11 The Technical Gauntlet™
- **Length:** 8-10 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Preparing for technical interviews
  - Practice problem-solving approach (think aloud)
  - Review fundamentals for your field
  - Mock technical interviews with peers

### 5.12 The Interview Types Guide™
- **Length:** 5-7 min
- **Status:** Not recorded
- **Key Points:**
  - Behavioral interviews: STAR stories
  - Case interviews: structured problem-solving
  - Technical interviews: demonstrate competence
  - Panel interviews: engage all members
  - Group interviews: collaborate and stand out
