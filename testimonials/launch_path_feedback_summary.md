- **Mohammed:** "The biggest change I experienced was gaining a structured and repeatable approach to both resume building and interview preparation."
- **Both:** Listed increased confidence and better stories as key transformations.

### Materials
- **Mohammed:** Resume transformation materials, story vault, story evaluation framework (2-star vs 5-star ranking system), and the application pipeline were all called out as valuable.
- **Osaid:** Interview practice materials rated highest. Pre-Session 2 materials (Story Vault Template) rated 5/5.

## 2. Negative Feedback / Pain Points

### Pre-Work Can Feel Tedious (Mohammed)
- "I said an 8 [on NPS] because I do believe that some of the 'pre-work' can be tedious and long and some may see it as that."
- **Nuance:** He immediately adds that "a majority of the homework is VERY beneficial." The issue is the initial perception of length, not the quality.

### Session 2 Was the Weakest Session (Mohammed)
- Rated 3/5 (lowest of all session ratings across both respondents).
- The pre/post Session 2 materials were also rated 3/5 by Mohammed.
- **Root cause:** The first part of the session was less valuable; the hands-on dissection of stories in the second half was the real value. Suggests the session front-loads theory too much before getting to practice.

### Job Search Tracking (Osaid)
- The job search tracker materials were "the least helpful" to Osaid specifically because clients are told to *create* the spreadsheet rather than being given a ready-made template: "Instead of just telling the people create the excel file to track the job search I would say giving a template of the excel file for them to use would have been helpful and would save them a lot of time."

### Materials Are Too Static / Not Interactive (Osaid)
- "The pre materials they are just an HTML page with valuable information in it of course but I would suggest make it a little more interactive so that the people aren't only reading. Make videos giving that same information."
- Wants multimedia (video walkthroughs) layered on top of the existing written materials.

### Materials Not Reviewed in Sessions Feel Wasted (Mohammed)
- "Materials that were not directly reviewed or applied during the sessions were less helpful, as they were harder to fully implement independently."
- If it's not workshopped live, some clients won't go deep on it alone.
