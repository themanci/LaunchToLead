üìπ Video Format & Technical Requirements

Recording Setup:

  • Orientation: Horizontal/landscape (NOT vertical like TikTok)
  • Resolution: 1080p minimum (most smartphones default to this)
  • Length: 2-4 minutes ideal (max 5 minutes)
  • Format: MP4, MOV, or similar standard video format

Lighting & Audio:

  • Natural lighting: Face a window if possible, or use a well-lit room
  • Avoid backlighting: Don't have bright lights/windows behind you
  • Quiet space: Minimize background noise (no fans, AC, traffic)
  • Clear audio: Speak clearly and at normal conversational volume
  • Test first: Record a 10-second test to check lighting and sound

Camera Position:

  • Eye level: Camera should be at your eye level (stack books under laptop if needed)
  • Distance: Position yourself so your head and shoulders fill most of the frame
  • Centered: Keep yourself centered in the frame with a little headroom
  • Background: Clean, simple background (home office, living room, nothing distracting)
üí° Pro Tip:

Use your smartphone on a tripod or propped up securely. Phone cameras are usually better than laptop webcams! If using your phone, make sure it's in landscape mode.
